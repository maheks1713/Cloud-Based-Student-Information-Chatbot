Our Facilities

As you walk around our campus, you will see a variety of academic buildings, residence halls, research centers, and recreational facilities.

World Class Library

Our library is housed in a modern building, designed to provide a comfortable and welcoming space for study and research. The collection at the Oasis University library includes over a million books, journals, and a wide range of electronic databases, providing access to thousands of e-books, e-journals, and other digital resources.

Largest Play Ground

Oasis University's playground is a popular spot for students to unwind and take a break from their academic pursuits. The playground features a variety of recreational facilities, including basketball courts, soccer fields, and a jogging track. The well-maintained grounds and scenic views make the playground an ideal place to relax and stay active.

Tasty And Healthy

Oasis University's canteen is a bustling hub of activity, providing students with a range of delicious and affordable food options. The canteen features a variety of cuisines, catering to diverse tastes and dietary needs. The friendly staff and welcoming atmosphere make the canteen a popular spot for students to socialize and refuel between classes.
